ATRIPPER

A journey into limitless beauty

ATRIPPER is a personal project that aims to show that artificial intelligence can be used to create images and videos with much higher quality and soul than the typical “AI slop” you see everywhere.

We use the most advanced AI tools available today, always working directly with them. Behind every piece there is real manual work: careful prompting, many iterations, and special attention to expressions, lighting, and atmosphere so that everything feels real and has presence.

It’s not automatic magic, but it is the best we can achieve right now with current technology. As the tools continue to evolve (and who knows how far they will go), this project will keep improving along with them.

The goal is simple: to create pieces that look good, have character, and are worth looking at. We are not chasing absolute perfection or filling the site with content. We just want to make things with good taste and real intention.

PauAi represents the softer, more luminous side. VikAi brings more strength and intensity. Together they explore a duality we enjoy, and we will continue developing it over time.

The Vision

We want to show that AI can be used to create digital art with real quality and feeling, not just quick and generic results. This project is our way of doing it as best as we can with the tools that exist today.

How We Work

Everything is generated directly with the most powerful AI tools currently available. The process involves a lot of testing, fine adjustments, and attention to detail. Nothing is 100% automatic. As the technology advances, we will continue improving the results.
